## Where to find the URL on Netlify

1. Open [app.netlify.com](https://app.netlify.com/) and select your site.
2. The URL is shown at the top of the **"Site overview"** page, e.g.:

   ```
   https://your-site-name.netlify.app
   ```

   You can also find it under **"Site settings → General → Site details → Site URL"**.

3. *(Optional)* Set a custom domain under **"Domain management"**.
